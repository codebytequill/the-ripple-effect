What Can You Do?

1. Share & Amplify

Share this tool on social media. Awareness is the first step toward change. Help others understand the stakes.

2. Learn & Support

Educate yourself further and support organizations fighting for disability rights. AAPD, DREDF

3. Advocate for Change

Contact your elected officials. Urge them to protect and expand job programs, Medicare, and Medicaid.

"Nearly 1 in 4 people with disabilities in the U.S. lives in poverty." - Center for American Progress
